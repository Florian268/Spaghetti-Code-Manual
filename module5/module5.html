Ethics and Inclusion

Ethics and inclusion present one of the largest issues in a software development space. As a software developer responsibility lies on your shoulders to ensure positive real world outcomes. Inclusion also relies on your participation and knowledge.
This training module will introduce you to the five components of ethics and inclusion. By the end of this module, you will be able to recognize exclusionary practices and unethical scenarios.
