Ethics and Inclusion

Ethics and inclusion present one of the largest issues in a software development space. As a software developer responsibility lies on your shoulders to ensure positive real world outcomes. Inclusion also relys on your participation and knowledge.
This training module will introduce you to the five components of ethics and inclusion. By the end of this module you will be able to recognize exclusionary practices and unethical scenarios.
Components of Ethics
  • Honesty
    • A developer should have code well commented and preform the function as stated.
    • A developer should not lie to the public when asked about functionality.
  • Voicing Concerns
    • If a developer feels uncomfortable about a project request voice the concerns.
    • If a developer would be required to preform an unethical action make a desicion on continuing participation.
  • Follow the Law
    • Follow the law as written as a basic rule of thumb
    • Law can sometimes be ambiguous and a project may come up that is legal but not following the spirt of the law in this case make informed desicion to continue participation.
Components of Inclusion
  • Accesibility via Universal Design
    • Ensure your product can be accessed from multiple device types.
    • Avoid all captial letters or camel case as many screen readers will read the letters individually
    • Label images
    • provide subtitles to audio
  • Inclucive Language
    • Avoid offensive language or imagery such as ablist, violent, sexist, racist, or classist language or imagery.
    • Utilize common english words to convey meaning
