UML Diagrams

What Are UML Diagrams

UML diagrams model and document software. There are several different types of these diagrams. In this section, we will go over some of the most popular types of diagrams and how you can implement them into your coding endevors.

Class Diagrams

Perhaps the most popular is the Class Diagram. This has boxes that label each class and its methods (marked by its privacy) and its connection to each other class.
