Conclusion

UML Diagrams display the complexities of software in an easily digestible format. Allowing programmers to share an abstraction of their software without having to read through hundreds of lines of code becomes a requirement when working in a team setting, and especially crucial in a corporate setting, where not everybody can understand code. There are numerous ways to visualize programs, but the ways in this module are some of the best at doing it.
